Over the past year, people around the world have begun to change the way they live, work, and connect with one another. Many of us spend more time online, communicating through video, messages, or social media, and this shift has created both opportunities and challenges. Some individuals feel closer to their friends because they can talk every day, while others feel more distant because they miss physical contact and real-life interaction. Despite these differences, most agree that understanding how to balance work, rest, and personal time is becoming more important.
